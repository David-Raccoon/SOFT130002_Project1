Title

In U.S. English there is also a preferred way to develop the main points. If a person is writing about scuba diving, with main points on equipment and safety tips, he or she is expected to develop the point on equipment by talking only about equipment. Safety tips should not be mentioned in the midst of the discussion about equipment.
